About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Trevor Coons and this portfolio is designed to showcase my work. I attended San Francisco State University and recieved a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology. I graduated in 2017 and went into staffing for a year before deciding to switch my career to sales. After a successful few years, the pandemic made me realize it was time to learn hard skills to remain marketable through turbulent times. Thanks to the University of California Berkeley Coding Bootcamp, I have been able to make that dream a reality.
